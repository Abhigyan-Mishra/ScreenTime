# Features
* Daily average (week hours/7)
* Overall average (overall hours/total days)
* Breakdown of usage over the day (when the device was unlocked and locked)
* How this week compares to last week (% difference)
* How frequently the device is unlocked (average time between unlocks)
* Longest use session of the day
* Time used after bedtime
* How today compares to the overall average (hours and minutes)
* Total time spent on computer over the week
